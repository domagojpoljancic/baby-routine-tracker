## Privacy Policy

Baby Routine stores your entries locally on your Garmin device.

This app does not collect personal data, does not transmit data, and does not share data with anyone.

This app does not use third-party services, analytics, advertising, or tracking.

You are responsible for managing your data on your own device, including backups, resets, and device-level data handling.

If you uninstall the app or reset your device, your locally stored data may be removed by your device.
